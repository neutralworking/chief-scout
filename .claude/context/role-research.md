### Midfielder (from dedicated Midfielder article)
- **Box-to-Box** — Gerrard/Lampard, covers both penalty areas
- **Mezzala** — Wikipedia has own section! "Half-winger", plays either side of holding player. Barella type.
- **Holding Midfielder** — three sub-types identified:
  - **Destroyer** — tackles, regains possession (Makélélé)
  - **Creator / Regista** — deep-lying playmaker (Pirlo, Xabi Alonso)
  - **Carrier / Surger** — wins back + advances (Yaya Touré) ← this is the "General" / "All-Action" type
- **Deep-Lying Playmaker / Regista** — Pirlo, Xavi. Own Wikipedia section.
- **Advanced Playmaker** — Totti, Özil, De Bruyne. Own section.
- **False Attacking Midfielder / Falso Trequartista** — drops deeper from AM. Niche.
- **Inverted Winger** — own section alongside Raumdeuter
- **Raumdeuter** — Wikipedia explicitly names it: "space interpreter" in German, exemplified by Müller, Dele Alli, Tim Cahill
- **False Winger / Seven-and-a-Half** — central player deployed wide, drifts inside (Isco type)

### Forward (from dedicated Forward article)
- **Centre-Forward / Striker** — traditional #9
- **Second Striker** — Wikipedia has own section. Deep-lying forward, "in the hole". Bergkamp, Totti, Rooney.
  - Italian: *seconda punta*, *rifinitore*
  - Also called: shadow striker, support striker
- **Inverted Winger** — historical, evolved into modern inverted winger/false 9
- **False 9** — Messi, Totti, Firmino. Drops deep to drag defenders. Own section.
- **Target Forward / Target Man** — Drogba, Giroud. Hold-up play, aerial presence. Own section.
- **Winger** — traditional wide forward. Includes inverted winger variant.
- **Poacher** — implied throughout but no dedicated section (lives on the shoulder, clinical finisher)

## Roles that are REDUNDANT based on Wikipedia
- **Fluidificante** — just an Italian word for an attacking full-back. Same as Overlapping Full-Back.

- **Traditional Winger** vs **Direct Winger** — very similar. Wikipedia doesn't distinguish.

## Key Wikipedia insight: "Carrier / Surger" holding midfielder type
Wikipedia identifies THREE types of holding midfielder:
1. Destroyer (Makélélé) — we have as Ball-Winner
2. Creator/Regista (Pirlo) — we have as Regista / Deep Playmaker
3. Carrier/Surger (Yaya Touré) — we called this "Destroyer-Creator" then "All-Action Pivot"

Wikipedia calls the third type a **"carrier"** or **"surger"** — a player who "wins back possession and subsequently advances from deeper positions." This is the **Midfield General** concept.

## Archetype Research Notes (for DoF)
User noted: "all action midfielder is the midfield General, but I feel that should be an archetype"

Current 13 archetypes: Controller, Commander, Creator, Target, Sprinter, Powerhouse, Cover, Engine, Destroyer, Dribbler, Passer, Striker, GK

A "General" archetype would represent the Yaya/Vieira/Gerrard type — the carrier/surger who does everything. Currently this player maps to Engine (intensity, pressing, stamina, versatility) but Engine doesn't capture the ball-winning + progressive carrying + shooting from distance that defines a General.

Potential archetype: **General** = tackling + carries + long_range + stamina (a destroyer-dribbler-striker hybrid)
This would distinguish the Yaya/Vieira/Gerrard type from the Kanté Engine (pure work rate) and the Pirlo Controller (pure passing).

### Key tactical points from formations article
- 4-4-2: "one central mid as holding, other more attacking" — our Anchor + Box-to-Box
- 4-4-1-1: "second striker plays in the hole" — validates our Second Striker role
- 4-4-2 diamond: "DM as deep-lying playmaker" — our Regista
- 4-3-3: "DM (#6) + two AMs (#8, #10)" — our Anchor + Mezzala/Box-to-Box
- 4-6-0: "centre forward exchanged for a trequartista" — our False 9
- 3-5-2: "wing-backs covering full flank" — our Wing-Back role
- 3-4-3: "no sweeper, three classic CBs, wing-backs more attacking" — modern 3atb
- Gasperini 3-4-3: "high pressure, speed, strength, one-on-one defending" — Atalanta system
- Guardiola 3-2-4-1: "begins as 4-2-3-1, inverted full-backs tuck in" — our Inverted Full-Back
- "Carrier/surger" midfielder type (Yaya Touré) explicitly named in Midfielder article — our Carrier role

### Formation → Role Slot Mapping (for formations page)
Key insight: each formation slot implies a tactical role. E.g.:
- 4-2-3-1 wide mids = Inverted Winger or Inverted Winger (not traditional winger)
- 3-5-2 wide players = Wing-Back (not winger, not full-back)
- 4-3-3 with DM = Anchor/Regista + 2x (Mezzala/Box-to-Box/Carrier)
- 4-4-2 diamond AM = Trequartista or Advanced Playmaker
- 4-1-2-3 lone CF = Complete Forward or Target Man (needs to hold up)
