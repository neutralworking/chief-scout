22-year-old Danish midfielder has added goalscoring to his range these past few months, regularly on the scoresheet for his table-topping club in the Norwegian Eliteserien and thrice netting in Conference League qualification, including goals against Bohemians 1905 of the Czech Republic andArmenian outfit Pyunik. Spright, skilful and determined in box-to-box mode, he also has experience as a left- winger, often occupying that role at previous club AGF of Aarhus. Great in one-on-one situations and plays with a lot of heads-up awareness. A first senior cap for Denmark surely cannot be far away.
