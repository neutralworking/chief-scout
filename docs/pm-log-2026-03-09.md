## Decision 1: Key Moments linked to News Stories

**Decision:** Player profile "key moments" (e.g., "Derby della Mole solo goal", "Derby d'Italia brace") should link to real articles from `news_stories`. Click opens a small modal with article content/summary. Not hover вЂ” click.

**Implication:** Requires a new `key_moments` table linking players to specific news stories with editorial labels, plus a frontend modal component.

---

## Decision 2: Personality System = Core Differentiator

**Decision:** The 4-letter personality code (INSP, ANLC, etc.) is the crown jewel. Must be front-and-center in the UI, not buried in a sub-panel.

**Implication:** Personality display needs prominence in player cards (list view), player detail header, and search/filter. The `player_personality` table (ei/sn/tf/jp scores, competitiveness, coachability) is the data source.

---

## Decision 3: Playing Styles alongside Personality

**Decision:** Playing styles/archetypes (13-model scoring) presented alongside personality as a paired feature: WHO they are + HOW they play.

**Implication:** Personality and playing style should be visually coupled in the UI. Data sources: `player_profiles.archetype` + `player_profiles.model_id` + the 13-model compound metric hierarchy.

---

## Decision 4: "Sophistication Simplified"

**Decision:** Design principle вЂ” the depth (confidence levels, environment suppression, evidence-linked grades) must be presented cleanly. Complexity lives underneath; the surface is intuitive.

**Implication:** Progressive disclosure pattern throughout the UI. Default views show simplified scores; drill-down reveals confidence, sources, suppression factors. Affects every component design.
