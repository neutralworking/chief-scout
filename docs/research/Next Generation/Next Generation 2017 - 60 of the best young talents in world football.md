CountryArgentina

**September 2022 update** After slumping into the Inter reserves earlier in the year, he decided to restart his career on loan at recently promoted Tigre back in his native Argentina. Quickly establishing himself as a regular in a high-flying Tigre side, he scored the winning goal in the quarter-finals of the league title race against River Plate. And this taste of success and regular football has helped him dream of bigger challenges. “I have been part of all the Argentina youth teams, but now my goal is to make the national team, the ultimate goal for any player.”
