PositionMidfielder

Last year Le Havre were raided by Liverpool for Yasser Larouci. Born on the outskirts of the Algerian Sahara, Larouci is a physical player with an explosive burst of pace and a good left foot. **October 2020 update** Progress of sorts for the young defender as he made his first-team debut in the FA Cup – and one further appearance in the same competition – but has started 2020-21 back with the under-23s.

**October 2019 update** Gómez has made occasional first-team appearances for Defensor, and was a member of Uruguay’s squad for the Under-20 World Cup in Poland.  
  
**October 2020 update** In January the forward moved on loan, with an option to buy, from Uruguay’s Boston River to Sassuolo in Serie A. They exercised that option in the summer and promptly loaned him to Albacete, the Spanish second division club owned by the same family that also owns Boston River. Albacete intend to use him initially in their reserve squad, but he may be involved in the first team as the season progresses.  
  
**October 2021 update** Plundered five goals in just 10 starts while on loan at Albacete’s second team and has since been promoted to the first team where he recently made his first start in a 2-0 over Real Madrid’s B side. “I felt really good out there,” Gómez enthused. “I’m getting a lot of support from my teammates and that’s really helping me out on the pitch. I hope the coach maintains his trust in me.”  
  
**September 2022 update** Albacete passed up on the opportunity to sign the young forward from Sassuolo in Italy despite Gómez featuring regularly last season for the Spanish Segunda División side’s senior team before seeing his form curtailed by injury. Now back in Uruguay with Boston River where he has so far only appeared off the bench.  
  
**October 2023 update** A much better year for the 22-year-old striker who has finally found some consistency at Boston River. “Ever since pre-season I’ve felt good, the team’s looking good and we’re very united,” Gómez noted back in March. “It’s a good group and that’s important because that makes a difference to me out on the pitch.” Has so far managed seven goals in 2023.
