No emphasis on tactics, just a belief that with the best players you win things. Going back to the 50s with Raymond Kopa Real Madrid have had a belief that they are the best football team in the world, that they should possess the best players, and that they should win in every game they play. Their players thus tend to be specialists, with larger than life personalities and distinctive, over-exaggerated playing styles. You can categorise Real Madrid players more easily than players from any other team. The prolific Ronaldo. The powerful Bale. The flamboyant Marcelo. 

Real Madrid are about superiority, beating you before you’ve set foot on the pitch.
Both first and second XIs are exceptional, as are the reserves.
Attacking is normally quick, they don’t really boss possession.
Attack power is always exceptional.
Shot supremacy.
They play at 25% until big moments, when they always execute. They are the arch executioners.
