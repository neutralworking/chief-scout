PositionMidfielder

Born5 January 2003

Born in the traditional rugby stronghold of Oyonnax in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region, Arli’s stock has been on the rise ever since he joined Dijon from local club Jura Sud Foot in 2018. The son of Turkish parents who moved to eastern France before he was born has been named in the matchday squad for several of his club’s matches in Ligue 1 so far this season but, at the time of writing, has yet to make his debut. It is surely only a matter of time, however, especially given recent reports that have linked Juventus, Valencia and Monaco with a move for the 17-year-old. Arli has yet to sign a professional deal with Dijon despite making the breakthrough to the first-team squad. "We have seen some interesting potential, now we must try to merge it with a professional approach,” admitted Dijon manager Stéphane Jobard in June. **[Ed Aarons](https://twitter.com/ed_aarons)**
