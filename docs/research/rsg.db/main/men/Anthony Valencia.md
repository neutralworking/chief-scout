
ClubIndependiente del Valle

PositionWinger

Born21 July 2003

A fast left-footed winger, who played a key role in Ecuador’s Under-20 Copa Libertadores title. Often plays on the right to enable him to cut inside and score from outside the area. He has explosive pace and is very good in one-on-one situations. Seems keen to improve at all times and that is a good quality to have. One of the youngest players (16) to have won the Under-20 Copa Libertadores he is now looking for more experience in senior football. **[Jaime F Macias](https://twitter.com/Jaimefmacias?lang=en)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Continues to knock in the goals for Independiente del Valle’s feeder club, Independiente Juniors, in the Ecuadorian second division and has earned rave reviews for his creativity and devastating ability to beat a man. Still awaiting promotion to the first team despite constant interest from Europe. But he’s in excellent hands while playing at Del Valle. His time will come.  
  
**September 2022 update** Made the move to Royal Antwerp this summer and quickly scored his first goal in the Belgian league in a 2-0 Europa Conference League win over Lillestrom. Recently won his first call-up to the national team for September’s friendlies against Saudi Arabia and Japan, but remains an outside bet for a place in Ecuador’s World Cup squad.  
  
**October 2023 update** Has bounced back from an injury-hit year with Antwerp last term to show more spark with the newly crowned Belgian champions this season. Made his Champions League debut against Barcelona in September, coming on for the last few minutes of their 5-0 defeat.
