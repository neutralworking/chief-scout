
ClubSchalke 04

PositionDefender

Born9 March 2003

Perhaps the most naturally talented player of his generation, according to many scouts. The youth international spent his early years at Mechelen before joining Club Brugge. Last year, in July, he decided to try his luck abroad and joined Schalke. A physically strong defender, he has been somewhat hampered by injuries and has had two operations in the past few months. Has a twin brother, Jordy, who also plays for Schalke. “The language barrier is a challenge for us but we are getting extra lessons,” they said early on. **[Kristof Terreur](https://twitter.com/HLNinEngeland)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Progressing well through the youth ranks at Schalke, with the club’s struggles at senior level suggesting he may get a chance to prove himself sooner rather than later.  
  
**September 2022 update** Was promoted to Schalke’s reserve team in the summer and made his debut in the Regionalliga (fourth tier) in July, playing the full 90 minutes against SV Straelen.  
  
**October 2023 update** Made 11 Regionalliga appearances in 2022-23, including a 5-5 humdinger against Borussia Mönchengladbach on the last day of the season, but was released at the expiry of his contract and has yet to find a new club.
