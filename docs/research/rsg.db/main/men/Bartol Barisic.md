
ClubDinamo Zagreb

PositionForward

Born01 January 2003

One of most prolific scorers in the Croatian youth leagues since Andrej Kramaric, Barisic could be seen as a cross between the Mauro Icardi and Harry Kane types. “He has the classic goal instinct,” says Jozo Bandic, one of the Dinamo Zagreb academy coaches. “He has the same desire when the score is 2-0, 5-0 or 9-0. His passion and hunger are incredible.” So you get the picture – but it’s not just the hunger, it’s also the variety of his clinical finishing and off-the-ball movement that makes him stand apart. Barisic can score from any postion and in any situation: be it tap-ins, headers, on the counter or beating his man in a direct challenge before unleashing the ball. Currently with Dinamo’s reserve team in the second division, he made a brief off-the-bench debut for the first team in July. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Has not made any visible progress since this time last year. Not anywhere near Dinamo’s senior side for now.  
  
**September 2022 update** Scored 10 goals last season for Dinamo reserves in the second division. Now on loan at Domzale in Slovenia’s top flight, which should definitely be a step up in his transition to senior football.  
  
**October 2023 update** Scored nine goals in 30 appearances for Domzale, but that didn’t earn him a return ticket to Dinamo. Instead the club terminated his contract and he left for Dunajska Streda in the Slovak top division.
