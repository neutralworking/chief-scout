If an award existed forthe most underrated playerin Ligue 1, the Lille central midfielder and skipper would be a prime contender. Most unlikely to be lit up in neon, neither showy nor spectacular, the 33-year-old former Ajaccio and Rennes stalwartis the personification of quiet effectiveness, someone who oils the Lille machinery with his metronomic passing, strength in the tackle, wide- awake shielding of the back line and excellent leadership. Without fuss or ego, he creates the framework for others to perform, such as the team’s attacking outlets Remy Cabella and Jonathan David. Lille coach Paulo Fonseca thinks it strange that Andre has never played for France.
