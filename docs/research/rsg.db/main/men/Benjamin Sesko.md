The next Erling Haaland or a Slovenian Peter Crouch? Well, probably neither, but Salzburg faced extremely strong competition, from Manchester City, Borussia Dortmund and Ajax, before their attempt to lure him from Domzale’s youth ranks ultimately proved successful last year. They paid around €2.5m for a player who was barely 16, but knew what they were getting. Despite his height (6ft 4in, the same as Haaland), he is fast and skilful enough to put any defender in trouble, while being very mobile and dynamic, often stepping back into a withdrawn role as well as pressing the defence when his team is not in possession of the ball. Sesko was a part of the Salzburg team that reached the Uefa Youth League semi-finals last season, playing among kids who were three years his senior and scoring three goals in the process. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Things seem to be going very well for the youngster, who has, at the time of writing, already scored six times in 12 mainly off-the-bench apperances for Salzburg across all competitions. He was also fully capped in June, just one day after his 18th birthday, and should remain a starter for Slovenia.  
  
**September 2022 update** There are still comparisons to a certain Norwegian although his development is nowhere near as rapid and his his scoring rate not as good (a goal every 180 minutes in all competitions by mid-September). And the “Slovenian Haaland” does not seem to mind: “I will listen and learn so I can be even better than he is,” he has said. Suitors do seem to believe him, up to point, and this summer RB Leipzig kept him within the franchise, but he is only due to move to Germany next summer.  
  
**October 2023 update** Has made a very promising start at Leipzig — at the time of writing, five goals in eight apperances across all competitions — but comparisons to Haaland, now already quite annoying, still can’t be avoided. “I’ve said this many times — I’m a completely different player,” he says. “We’re both the same height and fast, but our playing styles differ. I don’t want to be compared to anyone. I just want to be Benjamin Sesko and try to be the best around.”
