**Bernd Schuster** (born 22 December 1959) is a German former professional [footballer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Association_football "Association football") of the late 1970s through early 1990s, who won club titles playing for the Spanish sides [FC Barcelona](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Barcelona "FC Barcelona") (1980–1987) and [Real Madrid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_Madrid_CF "Real Madrid CF") (1988–1990). He played as a [midfielder](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midfielder "Midfielder") and was nicknamed "der Blonde Engel" (the Blond Angel). After retiring as a player, he managed a number of European clubs, including [Real Madrid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_Madrid_CF "Real Madrid CF"), taking them to the league title in the 2007–08 season.
