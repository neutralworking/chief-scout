PositionDefender

Born6 September 2003

The central defender has impressed at youth level, starring at the 2018 Under-18 Asian Championships as India conceded no goals in the group stage en route to the quarter-finals. He impressed again on loan at developmental club Indian Arrows before returning to Punjab, turning down offers from bigger Indian clubs, in May. Composed on the ball, a great reader of the game, and capable of excellent, fast, long throw-ins, the teenager has already been earmarked for a quick progression to the full international side. **[John Duerden](https://twitter.com/JohnnyDuerden?lang=en)**

**October 2021 update** Established himself in the defence of Punjab last season though all have to wait from until December for the new I-league campaign to start.  
  
**September 2022 update** The Virgil van Dijk-loving centre-back has been playing for RoundGlass Punjab in the I-League and helped India’s Under-20 team win the South Asian title.  
  
**October 2023 update** Earlier this year, the defender made the move to the top of the domestic game, joining Indian Super League outfit Chennaiyin.
