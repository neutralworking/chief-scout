When Bayern Munich arrived in Doha for a training camp in January 2016, all eyes were on a 15-year-old who was already 6ft 3in tall. So young was Früchtl at the time that he required the permission of his parents to fly to Qatar but the then Bayern manager Pep Guardiola had indentified the Bavaria-born goalkeeper as an uncut rough diamant. Since then, Früchtl has been learning quickly in every training session with Manuel Neuer, who has been deeply impressed by his potential successor. “His shoe size is 48 and two-thirds [UK size 13]: I checked it,” said Germany’s No1. “I can’t loan him my shoes. I think I was about a half a head shorter than him at his age.” Neuer’s long-term injury has seen Früchtl called up to the bench for Bayern’s first team as understudy to Sven Ulreich, which is why he will miss the under-17 World Cup in India. **[Michael Reis](https://twitter.com/donreisino?lang=en)**
 
**October 2018 update** Has been part of the first-team squads together with Manuel Neuer and Sven Ulreich and has been playing regularly for Bayern Munich II in the Regionalliga. Made his debut for the Germany Under-19 team in a victory against Slovakia.
 
**October 2019 update** Continuing to rack up appearances for Bayern Munich II in the 3. Liga and made his debut for the Germany Under-20 side. Was wanted by a few Bundesliga sides in the summer but decided to stay at Bayern. Neuer, after all, can’t carry on for ever, can he?
 
**October 2020 update** Well, the bad news for Früchtl is that Neuer is still Bayern Munich’s No 1 and to make matters worse, the club also signed Alexander Nübel from Schalke.
 
**October 2021 update** Has just recovered from a collarbone fracture and is hoping to cement his place as Bayern’s No 2 after a season spent on loan at Nuremberg, where is was on the bench 33 times but failed to make the pitch.
 
**September 2022 update** Left Bayern after eight years at the club in the summer to join Austria Vienna. Was given his Bundesliga-debut for the Bavarians when he was substituted on in the last league game of the season against Wolfsburg.
