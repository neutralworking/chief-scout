
ClubShkendija Tetovo

PositionWinger

Born21 November 2004

He wears the No 9 shirt but is better suited to the wings – as he can play almost equally well with both feet – or, perhaps, the second striker role. Fast, agile, intelligent off the ball and attractive to watch, he also possesses excellent technique and looks particularly deadly in attacking transition when he runs from deep or cuts inside from wide. Made his senior debut aged 16 years and one day, immediately getting on the scoresheet. He has since played 30 games for his club and scored seven goals, although there’s still vast room for improvement. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**September 2022 update** By mid-September, he has made 58 senior appearances for his club, scoring 10 goals and making five assists. He is also playing for the nation’s Under-21 team — and he only turns 18 in November. If he becomes more consistent and chooses his next club wisely, he could make it big.  
  
**October 2023 update** Had a sub-optimal season with Shkendija and has not made great strides at the start of the current campaign either. Also seems to have lost his place in the Under-21 national team squad.
