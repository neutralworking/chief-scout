CF		Striker		Everton	ENG	B	0.0	Matt Busby recalled how his timing in the air made him so difficult to defend against, and Arsenal full-back Eddie Hapgood recalled his strength, speed and great sportsmanship.		Much better known to the public as 'Dixie' Dean, a nickname he was known to dislike strongly, Everton legend William Ralph Dean was one of the most prolific goalscorers ever to play league football in England. With his playing career coinciding with the surge in goalscoring following the change to the offside law in 1925, he set goalscoring records which appear to be untouchable in the modern game.
