PositionAttacking midfielder

Born3 March 2004

The magnificently named Ullensaker/Kisa were in deep crisis in August 2020 and were drawing against Stjørdals-Blink when a certain 16-year-old made his debut with four minutes to go. Solberg scored the winner and, as it happened, that goal saved the club from being dragged into a second division relegation playoff. But it was not just the goal. The dynamic midfielder/winger did more than that. Solberg fast became an important player and his pure talent was obvious. Top Italian clubs took notice and Solberg met with Milan legend Paolo Maldini, but still decided to sign for Juventus academy this summer. “We nearly signed Erling Haaland when he was 17 so we want to make sure we don’t miss out on you,” Juve officials reportedly told the youngster. **[Michael Yokhin](https://twitter.com/Yokhin)**  
  
**September 2022 update** Back in Norway with Lillestrøm, having signed a contract until 2026 on transfer deadline day: “I am looking forward to being myself again,” he said. “I feel that I didn’t have that freedom at Juventus, so I am looking forward to having fun with my football again.”  
  
**October 2023 update** A large part of Solberg's 2023 Norwegian season has been ruined by injuries but he said in August that he was on his way back. "It has been frustrating to be on the sidelines, and it has been tough to watch the others but I am really looking forward to playing again." Has made the bench twice recently.
