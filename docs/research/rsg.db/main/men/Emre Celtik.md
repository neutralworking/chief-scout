Altinordu keep churning out talent. Two of the most highly-valued players in Turkish football, Caglar Söyüncü and Cengiz Ünder, are both graduates of the club’s youth system. Yigit Emre Celtik is part of the second wave of footballers emerging from the Izmir-based side and he looks very promising. The left-winger plays a similar style to Eden Hazard and has already broken into the first team this season, when it seems he will get the chance to showcase his talent. **[Emre Sarigul](https://twitter.com/Turkish_Futbol1?lang=en)  
  
**October 2021 update** Having broken into the Altinordu first team as a 17-year-old he made enough impression last season – despite having an ankle problem – to join Eredivisie side Fortuna Sittard. “I hope to make Altinordu proud and act as their ambassador abroad, I am going to work really hard for this,” Celtik said after completing his transfer.  
  
**September 2022 update** Was on the bench no fewer than 15 times for Fortuna Sittard but played no Eredivisie football before joining Polish side Podbeskidzie, first on loan and then on a permanent deal. There he has already made seven league appearances at the time of writing, all of them as a substitute.  
  
**October 2023 update** Cut his teeth in the Polish second tier last season for Podbeskidzie Bielsko-Biala, getting regular minutes without being a starter. The midfielder is back in the Super Lig now having joined Kayserispor over the summer on a permanent deal.**
