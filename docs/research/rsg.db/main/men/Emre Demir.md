Became the youngest player to score in the Süper Lig aged just 15, nine months and 25 days. In total Demir scored twice and provided one assist in 16 appearances for Kayserispor last season. Barcelona were so impressed they decided to snap him up before any rivals got involved. The left-footed attacking midfielder will join the La Liga giants next season and will be under contract until 2027 with a €400m release clause. Former Kayserispor sporting director Suleyman Hurma first spotted Demir aged just eight years old. “I am not surprised by his transfer to Barcelona, in fact, I’m surprised it took so long,” he says. “If he keeps developing at the same rate over there you are going to see the rise of a huge star”. **[Emre Sarigul](https://twitter.com/Turkish_Futbol1?lang=en)**  
  
**September 2022 update** Joined Barcelona this summer after a season with a few league and cup appearances for Kayserispor. He is currently part of the Barcelona B setup where he will still benefit from the advice of the first-team manager, Xavi. “Xavi was one of the best football players in the world and I am very thrilled to work with him and think I will learn a lot from him,” he said.  
  
**October 2023 update** In a bizarre turn of events Demir ended up leaving Barcelona after just six months to join Fenerbahce. Demir cited a change of personnel at Barça and struggling to settle behind his hasty exit. Fenerbahce loaned the youngster to second-tier side Umraniyespor where he will play first team football this season. Demir definitely has potential and is getting much-needed experience under his belt. The Yellow Canaries have nurtured some of Turkish football’s brightest talents recently so Demir is in a good place.
