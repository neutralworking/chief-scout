Rotundo grew up in San Diego where he also played futsal, something that no doubt contributed to his excellent first touch and control. He has also been praised for his vision and passing, and clearly does not lack in self-belief: “My ultimate goal is to be the best American player of all-time,” he told Soccer Nation in 2020. It may take some time for the Californian to break through in Germany as he is small for his age and easily hustled off the ball. Another young American star with international options, he is also eligible to play for France through his mother. **[Tom Lutz](https://twitter.com/tom_lutz)  
  
**September 2022 update** The Californian moved from the youth team at Schalke to the Under-23 team at Genk earlier this summer.  
  
**October 2023 update** Got playing time with Jong Genk and was part of the team that reached the Uefa Youth League quarter-finals, but has yet to feature this season.**
