Tall, right-footed and clever, Antzoulas is different from the usual perception of a dynamic Greek central defender. Capable of reading the game like a midfielder and even play there if needed, positioning and anticipation are his strongest skills, compensating for his lack of speed. Antzoulas signed his first professional contract in August, having guided the Asteras Tripolis under-20 side to the championship final, where he was scouted by European clubs. He has also established himself as a regular for Greece’s under-17s for the past two years and was the star performer in a surprise 1-0 victory over Spain in March. **[Costas Bratsos](https://twitter.com/CostasBratsos?lang=en)**
 
**October 2018 update** Made his professional debut for Asteras Tripolis in a cup loss against Atromitos and even though he didn’t play in Super League, Fiorentina Primavera took him on loan, with the obligation to sign him for €2.5m if he features for the first team.
 
**October 2019 update** Indispensable to Fiorentina Primavera’s first XI, he won the Coppa Italia Primavera and helped his team finish fifth in the league. He received a call-up to the senior squad, for three Serie A matches, against Napoli, Milan and Parma in May, without playing. Returned to Asteras Tripolis after his loan in Italy and is a starter in the Greek Super League 1.
 
**October 2020 update** Still awaiting his breakthrough although at least he played his first top-flight games of his career, making five appearances for Asteras Tripolis in the Greek Super League. Was also called up to the Greek Under-21 side.
 
**October 2021 update** Had a brief spell on loan at Cosenza in Serie B, during which he played in five league games but has not had much playing time since returning to Asteras.
 
**September 2022 update** A somewhat strange season for the 22-year-old central defender with very little action during the regular season, but then being called upon as a starter for most play-off games.
