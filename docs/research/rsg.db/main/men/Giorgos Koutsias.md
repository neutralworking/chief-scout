PositionStriker

Born8 February 2004

As an eight-year-old, Koutsias would travel for an hour every day after school to Salonika so that he could learn more about football at the PAOK Academy. "The car was my home. I slept, changed and studied there," he has said. Four years later he moved to the city, having chosen to follow his dream. Prior to the pandemic halting youth football in Greece, he played in 10 under-15, under-17 and under-19 tournaments and won the MVP and the top scorer awards in eight of them. In September 2020, still only 16, it was deemed that he was strong enough to make his professional debut in the Greek Super League and a few months later in the Europa League. A strong but fast attacker, with great anticipation and an eye for goal, he is the new jewel in PAOK's crown following Christos Tzolis. **[Costas Bratsos](https://twitter.com/CostasBratsos?lang=en)**  
  
**September 2022 update** Looking for his breakthrough season at PAOK, having already played in two European matches in the current campaign. Scored 10 times in 16 games in Super League 2 last season for the PAOK B team.  
  
**October 2023 update** Sold by PAOK to Chicago Fire in February for $2.5m plus add-on and has made a promising start to life in the United States, with three goals so far, including a magnificent winner at New York Red Bulls. Has also developed into a star performer for Greece's Under-19 and Under-21 teams.
