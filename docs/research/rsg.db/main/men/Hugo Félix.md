In his first game as a ball boy at Estádio da Luz, Hugo had the opportunity to hug his brother, João Félix, after the older sibling scored a goal. Now it is Hugo who dreams of making it to Benfica’s first team, the club he has been representing since 2016. He plays a little deeper than his brother, as an attacking midfielder, but is always looking for an opportunity to score. It will not be easy to follow João’s footsteps, but Hugo seems to have the right support, judging by the maturity he is showing and the competitiveness which which he approaches every game. “At his age, I didn’t have the physique he has,” says João, “and I didn’t do the things he does with the ball.” **[Nuno Travassos](https://twitter.com/nunotravassos?lang=en)**  
[Watch the moment the brothers embrace at the Estádio da Luz](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QTRAu6X3yUs)  
  
**September 2022 update** Was part of the Benfica team that won the 2021-22 Uefa Youth League and is now making the step up to Under-23 level.  
  
**October 2023 update** Continues to grow at Benfica, playing for the Under-19s, U23s and the reserve team. Played at the Under-19 European Championship, where he scored three goals and made two assists as Portugal got to the final, which they lost to Italy.
