Tshibangu is already starting for one of Africa’s biggest clubs and one of the continent’s best national teams. Although his skillset remains unvarnished, Tshibangu is extremely dangerous when he powers down the touchline, especially when there is space between him and his marker and he has the ball at his feet. In one-v-one situations, the winger is virtually unplayable and often draws fouls in and around the box. Like many young attacking players, the Congolese international can improve on his decision-making in the final third of the pitch, as well as his efficiency in front of goal. **[Maher Mezahi](https://twitter.com/mezahimaher)**  
  
**October 2021 update** The Congolese prodigy left TP Mazembe over the summer and signed with Anderlecht where he will continue his development under the tutelage of Vincent Kompany.  
  
**September 2022 update** Went from regular first-team football with TP Mazembe to barely featuring for Anderlecht reserves in Belgium. The frustrated winger opted for a move to Bandirmaspor – an ambitious club in the Turkish second division – in pursuit of more playing time.  
  
**October 2023 update** Only made nine appearances in the Turkish second division with Bandirmaspor before they cancelled his contract. The Congolese international then made a summer move to Al Nasr, a club in the top division of the Emirati league.
