
ClubHajduk Split

PositionAttacking midfielder

Born02 June 2003

A very intelligent, complete midfielder who has been a staple of the Croatia Under-17 team since his debut at 15 years of age. Since then, his progress has been linear with lots of room to build upon an extremely solid foundation. Makes quick decisions with the ball at his feet, is very good under pressure, technically brilliant and always seems to have the big picture in mind when delivering the ball. He is also an excellent taker of set-pieces. Contributes in both directions and can play in any midfield position, although the role of a modern attacking midfielder may be best suited for him. Can at times look a bit shy and not imposing enough to truly control a game, but that is something he can still improve on. Promoted to Hajduk’s reserves in the second division straight from the club’s Under-17 team this season. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Made his first-team debut for literally three seconds before being loaned out to second divison NK Dugopolje, where he is supposed to get regular playing time.  
  
**September 2022 update** Did well on loan to Dugopolje in the second division and returned to Hajduk. But, after three short cameo appearances, they decided to send him on another loan, this time to Varazdin in the top tier.  
  
**October 2023 update** Failed to impress at Varazdin and returned to Hajduk in January, getting occasional cameo appearances off the bench and scoring his debut goal for the club in May. Now a fringe player with dual registration, he’s able to get some playing time with NK Solin in the second division.
