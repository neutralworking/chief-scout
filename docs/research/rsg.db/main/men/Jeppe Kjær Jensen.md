There will be more to come, but Jensen has already set one record that will be impossible to beat (unless the Danish Super League change their rules). On his 16th birthday Jensen became the youngest player to appear in the top flight, coming off the bench for AC Horsens against Randers. He would have played his first game long before that but the rules stipulate that you cannot be younger than 16 to play in the SuperLiga. He soon scored his first goal and was invited to several clubs, visiting some of them before signing for Ajax in August 2020. “He has a unique vision and awareness. He makes the right decision nearly every time. He may lack some strength and physical power because of his young age, but his intelligence makes up for that,” says Bo Henriksen, his coach at AC Horsens, who is now at FC Midtjylland. **[Jesper Haue Hansen](https://twitter.com/jesperhaue)**

**September 2022 update** The Dane is currently not close to the Ajax first team but he is still very young and time is on his side.  
  
**October 2023 update** Made the move from Ajax to Bodø/Glimt in February before being loaned out to Sandefjord, also in the Norwegian top flight. Was not given many minutes at Bodø/Glimt but played 90 minutes in the league for the first time in 2023 on 1 October against Vålerenga and there is no doubt his parent club have high hopes for him with his contract stretching to 2027.
