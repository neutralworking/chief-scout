PositionForward

Born30 November 2003

The forward, who can play in the middle as well as on the wing, made an initial name for himself by scoring 17 goals in the 2018 Under-15 championships and impressed national team coaches with his fearlessness (an attribute much sought after in Chinese football) and a desire to run at defenders. A fine “Marseille Turn” against Kyrgyzstan in April not only created a goal but had critics purring. In November he became the third youngest player in Chinese Super League history, replacing Marko Arnatouivic for Shanghai SIPG. **[John Duerden](https://twitter.com/JohnnyDuerden?lang=en)**  
  
**October 2021 update** The 17-year-old has been loaned by his club to play for China’s under-19 team in the lower leagues and has been in various training camps preparing for qualification next year for Asia’s Under-20 Championships.  
  
**September 2022 update** The now 18-year-old joined Swiss club Grasshoppers in February and was immediately loaned out to NK Dubrava in Croatia.  
  
**October 2023 update** Still on the books of Grasshopper Club Zürich but the Chinese forward has been loaned out to NK Dubrava in Croatia.
