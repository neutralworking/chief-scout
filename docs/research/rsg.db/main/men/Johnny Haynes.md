He was able to find team-mates accurately over almost any distance and in any direction, instinctively knowing where they would be. Passing Range/Accuracy, Ambidexterixty (Passing), Influence	Famous as the first English footballer to earn £100 per week following the abolition of the maximum wage in 1961, inside-left Johnny Haynes is also remembered as one of the finest passers of the ball his country has produced.
