Joshua Kimmich 

  

  

Joshua Kimmich tried to take the lead but often wanted too much. He was the central build-up player in Bayern's game, as central defense lacked strong players. The 28-year-old took responsibility as a penalty taker and was involved in important goals. In terms of football, he is indisputably one of the best sixes in Germany. In the national team, however, he did not live up to the increasing responsibility and is one of the faces of the DFB crisis.

He does that really well""", Younes told to the media. During the U21 competition, Philip Röber of UEFA.com, described Kimmich's playing style on the website and compared him to İlkay Gündoğan. At Leipzig, he was part of an extremely attack-minded team in which his ball-winning qualities in front of a high back four shone through. In the Germany U21 side, he has a deeper role, but he has also caught the eye as a tidy defensive midfielder. Kimmich has great timing in his tackles, can dribble and does not mind putting in the legwork. Kimmich himself has cited Bastian Schweinsteiger as his inspiration.
