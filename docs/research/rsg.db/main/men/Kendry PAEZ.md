(Independiente del Valle)Among the many young players to have been purchased by Chelsea over the last two years, Paez’s name has probably slipped under the radar – not least because the Ecuadorianwill not complete his £17.2 million move to StamfordBridgeuntilheturns18in2025 – but his exploits for the national team mean he will not be an unknown name for long.In October, he became the youngest-ever goalscorer in South American World Cup qualifying after notching in a 2-1 win against Bolivia at 16 years and 161 days old. It was the latest in a growing list of records: Paezis already the youngest debutant and scorer in Ecuador’s top flight, having scored on his debut at the age of 15, while he was also on target in May to become the Under-20 World Cup’s youngest-ever scorer too.
