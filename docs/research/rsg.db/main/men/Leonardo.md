	WF		Winger			BRA	B	0.0			Leonardo started well-known as a left-back for the 1994 World Cup, keeping the young Roberto Carlos out of the team, much to his chagrin. He played well in the first games but was then given a four match suspension for elbowing Tab Ramos. Afterwards, Leonardo signed with the Kashima Antlers in J. League. He continued his success. In 1996, he returned to Europe, this time signing with French club Paris St Germain, where he again proved to be successful, one of his goals helping them to reach final round of the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup. At this point in his career, Leonardo moved into the midfield, sometimes on the left, sometimes as a playmaker. In 1997, Leonardo was given the #10 shirt for the national team. He was an important member of the team that won the Copa America in 1997. In the summer of 1997 he signed with Italian team AC Milan and became a prominent part of a star-studded lineup eventually.
