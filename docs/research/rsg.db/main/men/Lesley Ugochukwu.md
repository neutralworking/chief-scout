Following the departure of Eduardo Camavinga to Real Madrid, it has not taken long for the famed production line at Stade Rennais to unearth another jewel in central midfield. Born in Rennes, Ugochukwu is the nephew of former Nice, Rennes and Nigeria centre-back Onyekachi Apam and joined his local club’s academy at the age of eight. Also capable of playing in defence, the 17-year-old has been compared to World Cup winner Marcel Desailly for his tough tackling and versatility, earning rave reviews after making his Ligue 1 debut last season against Paris Saint-Germain. “He’s a very well-educated young man, surrounded by good people, and he never gets ahead of himself,” said former Rennes academy director, Landry Chauvin. **[Ed Aarons](https://twitter.com/ed_aarons)**  
  
**September 2022 update** Made 18 appearances in Ligue 1 last season and has been linked with Arsenal. “I have full confidence in him,” says Rennes coach Bruno Génésio.  
  
**October 2023 update** Joined Chelsea on a seven-year contract for £23m after a brilliant season that helped Rennes finish fourth in Ligue 1. Made his full Premier League against Bournemouth in September and earned praise from Mauricio Pochettino.
