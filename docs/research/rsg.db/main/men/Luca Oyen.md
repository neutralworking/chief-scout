
ClubKRC Genk

PositionAttacking midfielder/winger

Born14 March 2003

KRC Genk were over the moon when Luca Oyen signed a contract extention until 2022 at the end of October last year. The fear, as always with the biggest talents, is that they will leave too early. And Oyen, the son of the former Nottingham Forest defender Davy, is considered one of the greatest talents of his generation. With his superb technique, good vision and ability to do things at a high speed, Oyen has everything a modern attacking midfielder needs. He is also a hard worker and has a mature football brain. Oyen made his debut for Genk in August and his first start followed at the end of September. **[Kristof Terreur](https://twitter.com/HLNinEngeland)**

**October 2021 update** Yet to make a real breakthrough into the first team but has shown signs of his promise during the early part of the new season.  
  
**September 2022 update** After a strong 2021-22 season, in which he made 20 league appearances for Genk, disaster struck 25 minutes into the new season as he tore his cruciate ligament against Club Brugge.  
  
**October 2023 update** Returned to training in December and to competitive matches at the end of March and said in September that he was ready for the new season. "I had a good talk with the coach," he said. "He has every confidence in me, so I'll be fine. I have to keep working hard and do everything I can in training to get into the team."
