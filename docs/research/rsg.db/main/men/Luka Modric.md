Real Madrid are not the same side when Luka Modric is missing.

They slumped when he was out in January and February, while the Croatian was absent for both legs of the Champions League semi-final defeat to Juventus.

That was a huge blow, because Madrid struggle for fluency without his prompting in midfield.

Modric is a delightful player, scheming and probing with his passing, winning the ball back with his appreciation of space, and Rafael Benítez needs him to stay fit.

Madrid look lost without their playmaker and his injuries disrupted their flow. It cannot be a coincidence that they won nothing last season.
