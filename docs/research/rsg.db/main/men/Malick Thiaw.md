AC Milan: dethroned as champions in Italy, lost to Inter in the Champions League. The national team: last without words. And yet the young Malick Thiaw, who acted as reliable as he was courageous as a central defender, can feel like a winner in both teams in the past few weeks and months. The ex-Schalke player climbed into the international class of the kicker rankings almost out of nowhere thanks to his recent, remarkably good performances.
