Barcelona's rebuild continues but they don't need reinforcements between the sticks, with Ter Stegen taking home the 2022/23 Zamora Trophy.

When Manuel Neuer got injured in winter,  Ter Stegen became the number 1 goalkeeper for the national team.

This is offset by outstanding performances in La Liga and winning the championship.
