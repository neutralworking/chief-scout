
ClubPersebaya Surabaya

PositionAttacking midfielder

Born9 September 2004

After making his debut for Persebaya just two days after his 17th birthday, and becoming the youngest ever to appear for the club, his coach Aji Santoso said that the future for the teenager was extremely bright as long as he continued to work hard. The No 10, who can also play out wide, has everything necessary – balance, close control, technique – and the confidence to become a huge star in this football-loving nation. Perhaps guilty of trying to do too much at times, Marselino also, as his coach says, needs to slow down sometimes in terms of decision-making. That will surely come as will plenty more besides. **[John Duerden](https://twitter.com/JohnnyDuerden?lang=en)**  
  
**September 2022 update** Made his international debut and became Indonesia’s youngest ever goalscorer in June.  
  
**October 2023 update** The Indonesia international has headed to Europe to play for Deinze in Belgium’s second tier.
