Oddo was primarily deployed on the right as an offensive fullback, or on occasion as a wide-midfielder, due to his stamina and crossing ability. In his later career, he was deployed in a more defensive role as a centreback.[2][3] Despite being a defender, Oddo was known for his ability on set pieces and penalties.		He was part of the Italian squad that won the 2006 FIFA World Cup.
