
ClubRed Star Belgrade

PositionMidfielder

Born22 September 2003

When you start hearing more frequently about this kid, and chances are you will, some of the hype will likely be centred around his appearance: “Red Star’s blond Messi”, they call him, while others have compared him to the club icon Robert Prosinecki. Bacanin makes an instant impression both with his image and his elegance. The dynamic attacking midfielder, who signed a professional contract at the age of 15 despite interest from Barcelona and PSG, can control a game with his movement and silky touch. He retreats to the halfway line to collect the ball and is very good at distancing himself from his marker as well as creating chances through his dribbling skills and killer passes. Also a defensive presence, his long legs helpful in ball-winnning situations, so might end up being used in a deeper role. Played in the Uefa Youth League at barely 16 last year and is currently with Red Star’s under-19s, but could be set for a first-team debut later this season. **[Aleksandar Holiga](https://twitter.com/AlexHoliga)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Yet to make his senior debut for Red Star – although he made the bench a few times – he was sent out on loan to Graficar in the second division.  
  
**September 2022 update** Did not make enough of an impression while out on loan last season and was released on a free transfer without making a senior appearance for Red Star. Now at Radnicki Sremska Mitrovica in the second division.  
  
**October 2023 update** Did not make it at Radnicki either and is now at third-tier Jedinstvo Surcin.
