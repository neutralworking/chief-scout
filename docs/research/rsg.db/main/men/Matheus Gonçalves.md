**Club**: Flamengo  
**Position**: Attacking midfielder  
**Born**: 18 August 2005  

## Background
A Brazilian talent with a left foot, Gonçalves is known for his ability to go past opponents. He shone at Flamengo and featured with Brazil youth teams.

## October 2023 Update
Was one of Flamengo's best players but loaned to RB Bragantino after Sampaoli took over.
