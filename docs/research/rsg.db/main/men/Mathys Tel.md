**Club**: Bayern Munich  
**Position**: Striker  
**Born**: 27 April 2005  

## Background
Tel, a French talent, joined Bayern from Rennes. Set records for youngest goalscorer in Bundesliga and DFB-Pokal for Bayern.

## October 2023 Update
Became a cult hero at Bayern with three Bundesliga goals, including a late winner against Borussia Mönchengladbach.
