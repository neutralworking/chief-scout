to draw their last qualifier, thereby usurping their opponents for a place in the Cup of Nations finals.

Minteh is a man who knows how to make an impression: his debut at the highest level in Denmark was for Odense v Copenhagen in September 2022, coming on in the 83rd minute and scoring the winner less than three minutes later.

Moving to Feyenoord this season on loan from Newcastle, whom he joined in the summer, he has also quickly won over the fans in Rotterdam and been thrust into Champions League action by coach Arne Slot, who is predicting big things from the lanky forward.
