
ClubAcademie Mohamed VI

PositionAttacking midfielder

Born17 February 2003

There are several reasons for Moroccan football supporters to be very excited about the generation of talent born in 2003, which also includes the left-back Anas Nanah and the forward El Mehdi Maouhoub, but among them all Mohamed Amine Essahel stands out. The attacking midfielder is most comfortable floating in the pockets of space between the opposing defence and midfield lines. His spatial awareness is advanced for his age and he possesses a sharp eye for spotting runners at all angles. Essahel is your prototypical north African playmaker, and under the auspices of the Académie Mohammed VI in Salé he is on track to play at the highest level. **[Maher Mezahi](https://twitter.com/mezahimaher)**  
  
**October 2021 update** Essahel and his academy-mate, Anas Nanah, both signed on loan for Belgian club KAS Eupen, which is renowned for their talent development. Both are currently training with the reserves.  
  
**September 2022 update** Consecutive injuries sabotaged a loan spell in Belgium and have hamstrung Essahel's career. He still has the technique to play at a high level should he overcome his injuries.  
  
**October 2023 update** The last few years have been difficult for Essahel as he has struggled to stay fit. He has now returned to the Moroccan league with Union Touarga Sportif in a bid to relaunch his career.
