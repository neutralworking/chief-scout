The midfielder has already made his professional debut for Esteghlal Khuzestan in the Iranian league as well as the Asian Champions League. He was also an intelligent and influential presence as Iran reached the final of the 2016 Asian under-16 championships. Helped by the legendary Ali Daei in his formative years, his range of passing and vision are impressive for one so young and he is touted as a future captain of Iran. Clubs from Italy and Germany have been sniffing around and it is only a matter of time before he leaves. **[John Duerden](https://twitter.com/JohnnyDuerden?lang=en)**
 
**October 2018 update** The midfielder is playing for Esteghlal Khuzestan in the Iranian league.
 
**October 2019 update** After a total of 39 first-team appearances for Esteghlal Khuzestan he has moved to Saipa FC in the top tier after his former team were relegated.
 
**October 2020 update** Is currently playing in midfield for Iranian league club Saipa.
 
**October 2021 update** Got a dream move to Iranian champions and powerhouse Persepolis last October though has yet to establish himself at one of the biggest clubs in Asia.
 
**September 2022 update** The midfielder recently left Persepolis to sign for fellow top-flight Iranian team FC Nassaji Mazandaran.
