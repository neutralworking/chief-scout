Lecce

The 23-year-old Montenegrin national team striker has certainly looked the part since leaving Slovakian outfit DAC Dunajska Streda for Lecce in Serie A this summer, scoring in each of his first three outings for the Giallorossi, a unique feat in the Puglia club’s history. The former Red Star Belgrade and Zeta front-man is extremely powerful in the air and can finish with either foot.
