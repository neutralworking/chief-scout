**Club**: Club Brugge  
**Position**: Defensive midfielder  
**Born**: 5 January 2005  

## Background
Mbamba, known for his strength, speed, and dribbling skills, joined Brugge from Genk. He alternates between the first team and the development team.

## October 2023 Update
Joined Bayer Leverkusen, played Bundesliga and DFB-Pokal games under coach Xabi Alonso.
