CM					URU	C	0.0	Despite a long and successful club career which brought six league titles with Peñarol, Obdulio Varela is best remembered both in his native Uruguay and around the world as the inspirational captain of the team which stunned Brazil to win the World Cup in 1950.  Captain of Uruguay for 13 years, he also appeared for his country in their defence of that title in 1954 and Uruguay never lost a World Cup match in which Varela appeared. Playing at centre-half, Varela's style was a forerunner of a modern holding midfielder.
