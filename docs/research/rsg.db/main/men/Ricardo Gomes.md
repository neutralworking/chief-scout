CD					BRA	C	0.0			One of Paris Saint-Germain’s most accomplished defenders, Ricardo Gomes was a key part of the side that won a fair amount of silverware during the early 1990s. The towering centre-back won the French title during the 1993-94 season and had won the Coupe de France the season before. After four years in the French capital he left the club after making over 150 appearances and signed for Benfica. Since retiring in 1996 he has gone on to have a lengthy managerial career and has coached 14 teams.
