WF		Winger				C	0.0	A fast outside right with a straightforward way who earned himself the nickname “the Blitz of Horas”. Kress was instrumental in Eintracht Frankfurt’s rise to a European power in the late-1950s. Rarely seen scoring goals, his asset was drawing several opponents his way to create space for the inside forwards. A likeable player because of his modest and reserved character, he was the oldest player in the Bundesliga’s first ever season 1963-64 aged 38.
