**Club**: Independiente  
**Position**: Striker  
**Born**: 17 February 2005  

## Background
Born in Santiago del Estero, Argentina, Hidalgo moved to Buenos Aires for his career. Left-footed with an excellent understanding of the game, he signed his first professional contract with Independiente, with a release clause of $15m.

## October 2023 Update
Flitting between senior and reserve teams at Independiente, remained at the club despite rumors of a loan move.
