**Stefan Ortega**

  

Whenever Manchester City coach Pep Guardiola relied on the ex-Bielefeld keeper, the keeper withstood the pressure: whether in his three Premier League appearances (including the very important one against West Ham) or on the way to the FA Cup victory , Ortega Moreno convinced. Overall, there were too few appearances, but in the coming season he could get even closer to goalkeeper Ederson.
