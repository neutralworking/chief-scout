CF					YUG	B	0.0	His dribbling ability and fast body swerve could take him past opponents with ease, so much so that Ferenc Puskás by his own admission tried to imitate the style.  The powerful shot that he had developed while playing at centre forward meant that he remained a goalscoring threat wherever he played.		Stjepan Bobek was one of the finest exponents of the inside-left position and was cited by the great Ferenc Puskás as an influence on his game. It was the arrival of Hungarian Illés Spitz as Partizan coach that really transformed Bobek's career, as Spitz saw the potential that he had to be an excellent inside forward.  Bobek was moved to the inside-left position and one he was accustomed to his new role, showed himself to be a natural there.
