The 16-year-old winger is a regular in the Burnley youth team after making his name appearing for the under-17s and under-18s while only 15. Apart from exceptional pace Jumeau has the useful knack of scoring goals from midfield and has featured on the scoresheet on a number of occasions. Last season he set a youth team record for most distance covered, ending up with stats to match those of the first-team player George Boyd. Jumeau, whose father is French and mother Canadian, also has a younger brother in the Burnley academy. Olivier plays as goalkeeper for the under-15s. **[Paul Wilson](https://twitter.com/paulwilsongnm)**  
  
**October 2017 update** Tristan Jumeau is now in his second year as a full time scholar at Burnley's academy. He scored in the under-18s' 2-0 victory at QPR in September and is already forming a useful partnership with Michael Fowler, our 2017 pick.  
  
**October 2018 update** Continuing his football career in the United States after enrolling on a four-year scholarship at Georgia State University.  
  
**October 2019 update** Still in America, midway through a sporting scholarship at Georgia State University, living in Atlanta and playing regularly for the Georgia Panthers.  
  
**October 2020 update** Still on the first-team roster at Georgia State University.  
  
**October 2021 update** Still on the roster at Georgia State University, although did not play a game in 2020.
