**Club**: Athletico Paranaense  
**Position**: Forward  
**Born**: 28 February 2005  

## Background
Roque, a Brazilian prodigy, led Paranaense to the final of Libertadores da América. Discovered by América Mineiro, transferred to Cruzeiro, then Paranaense.

## October 2023 Update
Top scorer at the Under-20 South American Championship and agreed to join Barcelona for up to €74m.
