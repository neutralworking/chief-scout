**Club**: Liefering  
**Position**: Attacking midfielder

## Background
Born in Austria to Hungarian parents, Jano joined Red Bull Salzburg academy and was promoted to Liefering. Known for his vision, technique, and dead-ball skills.

## October 2023 Update
Progressed with Liefering in the 2. Bundesliga and made his debut for the Austria Under-19 side.
